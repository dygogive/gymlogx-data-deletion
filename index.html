Gym Log X Workout Routines

📱 Important: Gym Log X stores all your workout data locally on your device only. We do not have access to your workout information, and it is never uploaded to our servers.

How to Delete Your Data

Option 1: Delete All Local Data (Recommended)

To immediately and permanently delete all your workout data:

  1. Open your device's Settings
  2. Go to Apps or Applications
  3. Find and tap Gym Log X
  4. Tap Storage
  5. Tap Clear data or Delete data

Or simply uninstall the app to delete all data automatically.

⚠️ Warning: Deleting your data is permanent and cannot be undone. All your workout programs, exercises, and training history will be lost. Make sure to export a backup first if you want to keep your data.

Option 2: Request Analytics Data Deletion

If you also want to delete the anonymous analytics data we collect via Firebase:

📧 Send Data Deletion Request

Or manually email us at: gymlogx.team@gmail.com

What Happens After Deletion?

✅ Local data (workout history):
  • Deleted immediately from your device
  • Cannot be recovered
  • No data exists on our servers (never uploaded)
✅ Analytics data (Firebase):
  • Automatically deleted within 2–90 days
  • Can be manually deleted upon email request (processed within 30 days)
  • Already anonymous (not linked to your identity)

Data That is Never Collected

Gym Log X does NOT collect or store:

  • ❌ Your name, email, or phone number
  • ❌ Advertising Identifier (Ad ID)
  • ❌ Precise or approximate location
  • ❌ Photos, videos, or files
  • ❌ Directly identifying personal information
